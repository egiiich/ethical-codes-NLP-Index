Anti-Corruption and Bribery
TBS considers any form of bribery to be
dishonest, immoral, unacceptable, and
therefore, such behavior is prohibited.
The local law prohibits payment of any
money or providing anything of value to
officials having obligations or duties
relating to the public interest and public
officials for the purpose of obtaining,
retaining or directing business. All TBS
employees, officers, business partners,
agents or independent contractors acting
on behalf of TBS must abide by these
laws and may not offer or provide bribes
or other improper payments, benefits or
anything of value to obtain business or an
unfair advantage.
